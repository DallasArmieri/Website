How to  Survive Hatch Your Dragon:
Goal #3: Raising Your Hatchling!
Step 1: Watch your hatchling.
Step 2: Feed your hatchling.
Step 3: DON’T LOOK AWAY FROM THE HATCHLING.
Step 4: Wait for it to grow less bitey.
Behold the true face of evil!
Dragon hatchlings are the MOST diabolical creature to ever skitter across the earth. Do NOT touch, look at, or otherwise provoke these tiny demons. Also do not take your eyes off of them or their ravenous hunger will lead them to start eating something. Remember to watch the breath attacks!
Watch out for the Green's Acid spit.
Avoid the blue’s frostbite breath.
And of course don’t get burn by the red’s fire.
When feeding, do NOT place hands or feet near the beast!
Make sure to feed the hatchling every 2 hours.
Not safe around children, animals, hands, feet, or anything valuable.
