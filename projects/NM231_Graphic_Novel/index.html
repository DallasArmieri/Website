How to  Survive Hatch Your Dragon:
Goal #1: Getting Your Egg
Step 1: Find a lair!
Step 2: Avoid Moma!
Step 3: Grab that Egg!
To find a lair: find a remote, dangerous, or otherwise unfriendly place and poke around (advisably with something you are not afraid to lose). The more remote and dangerous the more likely you are to find a liar. Exercise EXTREME CAUTION!
Make sure to check any local mountainside caves.
Subterranean areas are also a (un)safe bet.
Volcanic hotspots are prime real-estate for dragons.
Do NOT enter any areas where the mother dragon might take you by surprise!
Do NOT attempt to take an egg while the mother is nearby!
Wait until you are sure there is no mother at the nest!
Take caution when delving into unknow areas.
